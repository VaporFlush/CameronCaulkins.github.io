About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Cameron Caulkins, a passionate game developer fluent in C++ and C#. Crafting immersive experiences and bringing worlds to life through code is my passion. From intricate gameplay mechanics to seamless user interfaces, I thrive on the challenges of game development.
blockquote>
In addition to my expertise in C++ and C#, I've delved into the realms of JavaScript and HTML to create this portfolio website from scratch. It's been an exciting journey learning these new languages, and it reflects my commitment to continuous learning and growth in the ever-evolving landscape of game development.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work!
